THE RULES

  • First rule : Choose your 3 characters at random for the first 3 different fights,
  • Second rule : The jokers, of which there are 3, can only be used for the first rule, you can change your character with it,
  • Third rule : After the first 3 fights, choose your single favourite character to do 3 more fights,
  • Fourth rule : the winner is the one with the most wins in total (the possibility of a draw).

NOTE: if when selecting characters (random or not) you don't have the character in the game and you don't have a joker to change character, you automatically lose
